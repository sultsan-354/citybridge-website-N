Every great villa starts with a perfectly prepared site. Our team handles soil surveys, excavation, and all groundwork — building the foundation of something extraordinary.

02 — Foundation & Structure

Steel. Concrete.
Engineering that lasts.

We lay reinforced concrete foundations and raise your structural frame — engineered to UAE soil conditions and Dubai Municipality codes, built to outlast generations.

03 — The Build

Walls rise.
Your vision takes shape.

Blockwork, roofing, MEP rough-in — our on-site supervisor manages daily progress. You get weekly photo updates and full transparency throughout.

Every detail crafted.

Custom joinery, Italian marble, bespoke lighting — our fit-out team turns raw structure into a home that reflects your taste, your lifestyle, your family.

Your dream home.

Final walkthrough, snag-free handover, and keys in your hand. The beginning of a relationship — we remain available for maintenance and support long after you move in.

Our story

20 years of building
Dubai families' homes.

City Bridge Contracting LLC was founded in 2004 with a single purpose: to build premium villas in the UAE with the quality, honesty, and precision that clients deserve.

Over two decades later, we have completed more than 120 villa projects across Dubai — each one built with the same care we brought to the very first.

We work closely with every client from foundation to finishing — managing design, engineering, permits, and on-site supervision so your home matches your vision, budget, and lifestyle.
